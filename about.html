Get to know me

I’m David A. Kimmel, a seasoned Radar Image Scientist and Geospatial Intelligence professional with a passion for using data and technology to solve complex, mission-critical challenges.

With expertise in Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR) and advanced spatial modeling, I bring over a decade of experience in transforming data into actionable intelligence.
